Trading relations between EU and UK

Two interest questions after Brexit: (1) which country has the most impact in trading with UK, and (2) which country has the most to lost?

Imports and exports between EU27 and UK in 2015 (all products)

A statistical analysis about imports and exports (all products) between EU27 and UK shows some interest facts. The total import values over 182 billion euro, while toal export values around 316 billion euro. Undoulbtedly that Germany has the most benefit in exports (28.19%) to UK. It approximates the sume of three countries: Netherlands, France, and Ireland.

Source: European Commission

Brexit Sensitivity Index

According to S&P Global ratings, Ireland, Malta, Luxembourg, and Cyprus are the most vulnerable countries to any changed tratergies of the Brexit. The survey was conducted of 20 countries most exposed to the risk. The geomap shows measures goods and services exports to the UK, and bidirectional migrant flows. In this list, Canada (the only country that is not in the Eurozone), is the most exposed to the Brexit aftermath.

Source: S&P Global
